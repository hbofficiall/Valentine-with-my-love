For My Beloved Wife

My dearest Emaan Bukhari,

Tum meri jaan ho, meri pyaari biwi, meri raani, meri har khushi ka sahar... tum sirf meri wife nahi ho, tum toh meri zindagi ka har lamha ho. Tum itni khaas ho ke har pal tumhein kho kar sochun toh darr lagta hai... par yaqeen rakhna, main tumhein kabhi nahi kho sakta, kyunki tum mere dil ka woh hissa ho jo kabhi alag nahi ho sakta.

Haan janu, kabhi kabhi tum ghusa kar jaati ho, main bhi shayad kuch keh deta hoon... yeh life ka hissa hai. Husband-wife ke beech aise lamhe aate hain, thoda udaas, thoda dukhi... par yeh sirf ek pal hota hai. Asli haqeeqat yeh hai ke hum dono ek doosre se itna pyaar karte hain ke har gham bhi humari mohabbat ke aage chhoti si baat lagti hai. Tumhari har ghusai, har aansoo... uske peeche bhi wohi gehra pyaar chhupa hota hai jo tum mujhse karti ho.

Meri jaan, kabhi himmat na haaro. Tum bohat achhi ho, bohat samjhdar ho, bohat powerful ho. Tumhari andar woh taqat hai jo har mushkil ko aasan bana deti hai. Kabhi kabhi lagta hai sab kuch mushkil ho raha hai, raaste andhere hain... par yaad rakhna ek baat: jab bhi hum aise waqt mein hote hain, humein woh shaks yaad aata hai jisse hum sachche dil se pyaar karte hain. Uski soch, uska chehra, uska pyaar... bas itna yaad karte hi hum phir se strong ho jaate hain, muskuraane lagte hain.

Janu, tum bhi aisa karo. Jab bhi koi ghalat soch aaye, jab bhi lagay ke sab kuch bigad raha hai... bas meri yaad kar lena. Sochna ke tumhari zindagi mein ek aisa banda hai jo tumhe har haal mein pyaar karta hai, tumhare liye dua karta hai, tumhare liye jeeta hai. Sirf yeh soch lo... aur dekho, sab kuch theek ho jayega. Har dard halka pad jayega, har pareshani door bhag jayegi.

Tum meri Emaan ho... meri pehchaan ho. Ek din sab theek ho jayega, balki har din hum dono ke liye khoobsurat ho raha hai. Bas ek doosre ko tham kar chalna hai... pyaar se, bharosa rakhte hue.

Tumhari har saans meri dua hai,
Tumhara har lamha mera armaan hai,
Tum meri Emaan ho... meri jaan,
